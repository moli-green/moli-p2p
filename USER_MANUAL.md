## 1. The Philosophy: Campfire vs. Library

## 5. FAQ

**Q: If I close the tab, does my broadcast disappear?**
A: Yes. Unless someone else has Pinned it. If 10 people Pin it, the image survives as long as *any one of them* is online.

**Q: Can I access my gallery from another device?**
A: No. Your Identity lives in your browser's local storage. There is no cloud to sync with.

**Q: Who can see my images?**
A: Anyone connected to the mesh at the same time as you. Moli P2P is a public space. Do not broadcast secrets.

**Q: Is it safe?**
A: Moli P2P connects you directly to peers. IP addresses are exchanged to make the connection (just like BitTorrent). Use a VPN if anonymity is critical for you.
